Frequently Asked Questions

Is SmartStoxVest really free?

Yes! While we’re in beta, all features are completely free to use. We’ll notify users well in advance before introducing any pricing tiers.

What markets does SmartStoxVest support?

Currently, we support LSE, NASDAQ, NYSE, NSE, and major cryptocurrencies. More markets will be added soon.

Is my financial data safe?

Absolutely. We don’t store personal investment data. All analysis is done in real-time based on public data sources and your inputs.

How often are predictions updated?

Predictions and analytics are refreshed every time you run a strategy. Our models use the latest available market data.

Can I suggest a feature or report a bug?

Yes please! Use the form in the Join Us section or email us directly. We love feedback.
